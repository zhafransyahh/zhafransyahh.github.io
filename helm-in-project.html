01 ‚Äî Overview

What is Helm-In?

Helm-In is a smart helmet rental app built for BINUS University students. The challenge: riding to campus on a motorbike is the default for many Indonesian students, but carrying a helmet around campus all day is a hassle ‚Äî and not having one means unsafe rides.

Helm-In solves this by placing smart helmet lockers around the BINUS campus. Students scan a QR code via the app, rent a helmet from the nearest station, ride safely, and return it at any locker. Credits-based pricing keeps it affordable.
